Our Story

Charity: water was founded in 2006 by Scott Harrison, who wanted to make a real difference in the world after leaving a career in nightlife. He saw that millions of people around the globe were living without access to clean water, and he knew something had to change. The organization was created to bring safe, clean drinking water to communities in need, funding wells, rainwater catchments, and other sustainable water projects.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina is able to support her family and is healthier and happier.
